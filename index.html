    How to take care of pets?

    To take care of a pet, you can provide them with food, water, shelter, exercise, and regular veterinary care. You can also make sure they are properly identified and spayed or neutered.
    Food and water:
    Provide your pet with a balanced diet of nutritious food.
    Make sure your pet has access to fresh water at all times.
    Shelter:
    Provide a safe, cozy shelter for your pet.
